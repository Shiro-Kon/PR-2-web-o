Our Burgers

Shrimp Delight

Description: Delicious shrimp burger served on whole grain buns with avocado, cucumber, lettuce, and special sauce.

Ingredients: Whole grain buns, avocado, shrimp, cucumber, lettuce, special sauce.

Cheese Lover

Description: A mouthwatering burger loaded with various types of cheese, served on a freshly baked bun with lettuce, tomato, and a tangy sauce.

Ingredients: Freshly baked bun, beef patty, cheddar cheese, Swiss cheese, mozzarella cheese, lettuce, tomato, sauce.

Chicken Pineapple Burger

Description: Tender chicken patty topped with grilled pineapple, served on a soft bun with lettuce, tomato, and a zesty pineapple sauce.

Ingredients: Soft bun, chicken patty, grilled pineapple, lettuce, tomato, pineapple sauce.

Juicy Beef Burger

Description: A classic beef burger made with premium beef, topped with crispy bacon, melted cheese, lettuce, tomato, and a creamy sauce.

Ingredients: Brioche bun, beef patty, bacon, cheddar cheese, lettuce, tomato, sauce.

Quadruple Burger

Description: The ultimate burger experience with four juicy beef patties layered with cheese, lettuce, tomato, pickles, onions, and special sauce.

Ingredients: Sesame seed bun, four beef patties, American cheese, lettuce, tomato, pickles, onions, special sauce.

Vegetarian Delight

Description: A satisfying vegetarian option featuring a hearty veggie patty, served on a multigrain bun with fresh toppings and a savory sauce.

Ingredients: Multigrain bun, veggie patty, avocado, lettuce, tomato, cucumber, special sauce.
